What is Low-Fi video?

  • Content made using straight-forward, simple techniques
    •
  • Quick and easy to produce
    •
  • Not necessarily perfect
    •

Consider the purpose of your videos

  • Shorter videos are more engaging (and easier to re-make if necessary)
    •
  • Prepare and plan what you will say and how you will show this in the video

  • Be natural, be yourself and speak with enthusiasm

Making engaging content

  • Educational videos work best when they address a finite concept or skill.
    •
  • Implement diagrams, animations and visual imagery in addition to text.
    •
  • Support what you show with what you say.
    •

Recording high quality AUDIO is

important

Viewers will tune out if they cannot hear you.

When recording, do so in a controlled, closed environment.

Equipment

A USB microphone is desirable.

Test audio quality prior to recording.

If filming, consider lighting.
